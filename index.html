Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 baguette or Italian bread, sliced
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Mix chopped tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Slice the baguette and brush with olive oil.
  4. Bake for 8-10 minutes until toasted.
  5. Top the toasted bread with tomato mixture and serve.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 12 large button mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Remove stems from mushrooms and chop them finely.
  3. Mix chopped stems, cream cheese, Parmesan, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff mushroom caps with the mixture.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes until golden.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken thighs, cubed
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • Salt and chili to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in oil until fragrant.
  2. Add curry powder and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in coconut milk, simmer for 20-25 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and chili, serve with rice.

Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Melt chocolate over a double boiler; let it cool slightly.
  2. Whisk egg yolks into the chocolate.
  3. Whip heavy cream until soft peaks form.
  4. Beat egg whites with sugar until stiff peaks.
  5. Fold whipped cream into chocolate mixture, then fold in egg whites gently.
  6. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
